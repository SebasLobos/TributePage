Son Goku

The greatest warrior and Earth's greatest defender

Goku´s life time line:

  • 737 - Goku (Kakarot) was born. Bardock (Goku´s father) returns to Planet Vegeta on Frieza's orders and sends his son Kakarot to the distant planet Earth.
  • 749 - Goku become Master Roshi's students.
  • 750 - The 21st World Martial Arts Tournament takes place. Jackie Chun (Master Roshi undercover) wins. Goku takes second place.
  • 753 - The 22nd World Martial Arts Tournament takes place. Tien Shinhan wins. Goku comes in second place.
  • 753 - Goku drinks the Sacred Water and kills King Piccolo. Piccolo gives birth to his reincarnation, Piccolo Jr.
  • 756 - The 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament takes place. Goku defeats Piccolo Jr. and wins the tournament.
  • 761 - Raditz arrives on Earth. Goku and Raditz are killed by Piccolo.
  • 762 - Goku arrives at King Kai's Planet to be trained.He is resurrected after completing his training under King Kai and defeated Vegeta.
  • 762 - Goku transforms into a Super Saiyan for the first time and defeats Frieza
  • 767 - Goku battles Cell, how activates the bomb inside of him and Goku sacrifices himself by teleporting himself and Cell to King Kai's Planet.
  • 767 - Goku visits Grand Kai's Planet and participates in the Afterlife Tournament. Goku fights Pikkon and the fight ends in a draw.
  • 774 - Kid Buu destroys the Earth. Goku and Vegeta battle Buu on the world of the Kais. Porunga restores the Earth.Goku destroys Buu with the Super Spirit Bomb.
  • 778 - Beerus (god of destruction) meet Goku. Goku transform into a Super Saiyan God but is defeated by Beerus.
  • 779 - Goku train with Whis on Beerus' planet and he learn to become Super Saiyan Blue. Frieza is revived but Goku defeated him.
  • 780 - The Tournament of Power take place. Goku completes the Autonomous Ultra Instinct form and engages Jiren at full power.
